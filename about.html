This site was created with two main starting goals:

  • Have a place to store details of the Japanese lyrics. My japanese is pretty poor, but I like to try to get a better understanding of the original lyrics, to go farther than the sometime so-so English version. I might as well share the resources I am using.
  • Provide a place with some information about the band. There is no main page for the band at this point. I found this quite frustrating when I started to look for information about Nek!. So this site contains the base information about the band and their music.
